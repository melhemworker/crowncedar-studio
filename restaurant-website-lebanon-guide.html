What should a restaurant website in Lebanon include?

A restaurant website checklist covering menu, photos, location, hours, reservations, delivery links, SEO, and WhatsApp order flow.

A restaurant website in Lebanon should answer the questions customers ask before visiting or ordering: what is the menu, where are you located, are you open, what does the place look like, and how do I reserve or order?

The most important sections are menu, signature dishes, photo gallery, opening hours, Google Maps, reservations, delivery links, FAQ, and WhatsApp CTA.

Do not hide the contact path. Every important section should make booking or ordering easy on mobile.
