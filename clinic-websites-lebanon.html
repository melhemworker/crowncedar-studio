What a clinic website should prove

  • Who the clinic or doctor is, what services are offered, where the clinic is located, and how to book.
  • Original photos, doctor profile, treatment categories, patient FAQ answers, and clear contact paths.
  • A serious first impression for patients comparing clinics through Google, Instagram, or referrals.

Recommended clinic website sections

  • Hero with clinic specialty, city, trust point, and appointment button.
  • Doctor/profile block, services, before/after or case gallery when legally and ethically appropriate, location, hours, insurance/payment notes, and FAQs.
  • WhatsApp messages designed for appointment requests, treatment questions, and consultation booking.

Responsible medical website rules

  • Avoid fake testimonials and unsupported medical claims.
  • Use real clinic photos, real credentials, and accurate service information.
  • Make emergency limitations, appointment flow, and contact details clear.
